<h2>About Hair Transplants</h2>
<p>Now that we've seen who's had a hair transplant, let's discuss what it entails. Hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves moving hair follicles from one part of the body (the donor site) to a bald or balding part of the body (the recipient site).</p>

<h3>Types of Hair Transplants</h3>
<p>There are two main types of hair transplants: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). The FUE procedure is less invasive and leaves no visible scarring, making it a popular choice among celebrities.</p>

<h3>Recovery and Results</h3>
<p>Recovery from a hair transplant surgery typically takes a few weeks. The transplanted hair will usually begin to grow after three to four months. The quality of the results depends on the skill and experience of the surgeon, as well as the patient's hair characteristics.</p>
